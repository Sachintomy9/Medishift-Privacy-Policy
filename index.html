Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Welcome to Medishift. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our Application, we may require access to certain features of your device to provide our services effectively. This includes, but is not limited to:

Camera Access

Our application requires access to your device's camera (android.permission.CAMERA). We use the camera solely to allow you to capture photos for your profile, scan necessary identification documents, or upload required shift-related materials directly within the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

The images captured using your device's camera are used strictly for:

  • Verifying your identity as a healthcare professional.
  • Updating your account profile picture.
  • Submitting necessary documents for shifts or clinic requirements.

We do not capture background video, audio, or use the camera for any other purpose without your explicit consent.

3. Data Security and Sharing

We take appropriate security measures to protect your data. Images and documents uploaded via the camera are securely transmitted and stored on our servers. We do not sell your personal data or photos to any third parties. They are only shared with clinics or authorized personnel as strictly required to fulfill our service.

4. Your Choices

You can revoke camera permissions at any time through your device's settings. However, please note that doing so may prevent you from using certain essential features of the Medishift app that require photo submission.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact our support team at Medishift.
